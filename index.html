Act 1, Part 1

  • For 3 extra AP: Beat the boss at the shrine.
  • For 3 extra AP: Finish the fight against the old rivals within 40 turns.

Act 1, Part 2

  • For 3 extra AP: Beat the boss at the shrine.
  • For 3 extra AP: Finish the fight on top of the tower within 40 turns.

Act 1, Part 3

  • For 3 extra AP: Beat the boss at the shrine.
  • For 2 extra AP: Win the race.
  • For 3 extra AP: The battle can´t be won, but the loudmouth can be defeated.
  • For 3 extra AP: In Ymir, get his HP under 50% within 40 hours.

Act 2, Part 1

  • For 2 extra AP: Talk to Becky at Celdic - House to have her join the Courageous.

Act 2, Part 2

  • For 2 extra AP: Before going to the Sachsen Iron Mine, visit the Schwarzdrache Fortress by taking the Nortia highway.
