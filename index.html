The Legend of Heroes: Trails of Cold Steel II

Playthrough

To keep this list as spoiler-free as possible, I'm providing hints rather than concrete tasks to complete. These should be vague enough to keep you puzzled and make sense once you reach that segment in the game. If you still have questions, feel free to ask me on Twitter or so.

Prologue

  • Good to know: See what Clear Data carries over in the FAQ.

Act 1, Part 1

  • Good to know: See how bonding works in the FAQ.
  • Good to know: Whichever non-Class VII party member you bond with the most during Act 1 determines what accessory you will get in Act 2.
  • For 3 extra AP: Beat the boss at the shrine.
  • For 3 extra AP: Finish the fight against the old rivals within 40 turns.

Act 1, Part 2

  • For 3 extra AP: Beat the boss at the shrine.
  • For 3 extra AP: Finish the fight on top of the tower within 40 turns.

Act 1, Part 3

  • For 3 extra AP: Beat the boss at the shrine.
  • For 2 extra AP: Win the race.
  • For 3 extra AP: The battle can´t be won, but the loudmouth can be defeated.
  • For 3 extra AP: In Ymir, get his HP under 50% within 40 hours.

Act 2, Part 1

  • For 2 extra AP: Talk to Becky at Celdic - House to have her join the Courageous.

Act 2, Part 2

  • For 2 extra AP: Before going to the Sachsen Iron Mine, visit the Schwarzdrache Fortress by taking the Nortia highway.

    How does the checklist status get saved?

    The checklist is saved to your browser's local storage. Be careful when clearing your browser's cache as it will also destroy your saved progress.

    What carries over from the first game?

    Based on your level:

    LV 69 or lower:
    • 100 of each sepith
    • Attack 1 Quartz (STR +15)
    • Zeram Powder
    LVL 70-79:
    • 300 of each sepith
    • Attack 1 Quartz (STR +15)
    • Soul Blur Quartz (Faint 30% chance)
    • Zeram Powder
    LVL 80 and higher: 500 of each sepith
    • Attack 1 Quartz (STR +15)
    • Soul Blur Quartz (Faint 30% chance)
    • Saint Force Quartz (STR/DEF +25%)
    • Zeram Powder

    Academic rank bonuses

  • A0: Golden Emblem (HP +1000, EP+100, STR/DEF/ATS/ADF +10)
  • A1: Silver Emblem (HP +500, EP+50)

    So. How does bonding work in this game?

    Link Levels

  • Level 1: 0 pt.
  • Level 2: 1000 pt.
  • Level 3: 2000 pt.
  • Level 4: 3000 pt.
  • Level 5: 4500 pt.
  • Level 6: 6000 pt.
  • Level 7: 7500 pt.

    • General remarks

  • The choice of hot spring partner gets +600 link points.
  • A game of Blade II nets the partner +100 link points.
  • Your partner upon winning a Valimar battle gets +100 link points.
  • At the end of Chapter 1, everyone gets +500 link points. (NG only)
  • At the end of Chapter 2, everyone gets +1000 link points. (NG only)
  • Requirements to trigger the special someone bond event:
    1. Have at least 5000 points with this person.
    2. On 12/30, play Blade II with this person, get +800 points.
    3. On 12/30, engage in the Valimar battle with this person as co-pilot, +800 points.
    4. Make an appointment with this person (marked by a yellow exclamation mark.
